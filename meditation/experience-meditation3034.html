A Meditation on Freedom

May 11, 2010

Most people think freedom is the ability to come and go as I please. But true freedom means I can go beyond the stories of my daily life , beyond the illusions of the world and into a dimension of stillness... into an experience of my true self. It means that whenever I wish, I can travel freely between the drama of life and the stillness of my spiritual identity.
